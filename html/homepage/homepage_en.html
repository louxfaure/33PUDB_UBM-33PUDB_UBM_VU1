Search advices

When searching Babord+ the following operators can help to narrow or broaden your search :
* (truncation) enter an asterisk * at the end of a word to perform a multiple character wildcard search.
Ex : educat* (educate, educator, education,etc.).
"..." to search for an exact phrase type quotation marks around the phrase.
Ex : "carbon nanotube" .

Did you know ?

Think about locking your filters to keep on using them with your next searches.
Filters can include or exclude results.
